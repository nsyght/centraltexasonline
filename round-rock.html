About Round Rock

Round Rock has transformed from a small bedroom community into a full-fledged city with a distinct identity. The historic downtown along Mays Street is experiencing a renaissance, while new development along University Boulevard and the SH-45 corridor brings national brands and local favorites alike. The city sits at the intersection of Williamson County's explosive growth and proximity to Austin, making it one of the most strategically located and economically vibrant communities in Central Texas.
