💬 About Hand & Stone Massage and Facial Spa - Round Rock

Hand & Stone Massage and Facial Spa in Round Rock has built one of the largest spa review bases in Williamson County, earning 4.6 stars across 2,109 reviews by making professional massage therapy and skincare accessible through a membership model. The spa services cover the full professional massage range: Swedish relaxation, deep tissue therapeutic, hot stone therapy, prenatal massage, and couples massage. The facial program includes customized facials with skin analysis, chemical peels, microdermabrasion, and CBD-infused treatments. The monthly membership converts what most people treat as an occasional treat into a regular wellness practice. For Round Rock residents who want professional massage or facial care without the intimidation of a high-end resort spa, Hand & Stone provides a reliable, high-quality option.
