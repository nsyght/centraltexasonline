About Georgetown

Georgetown has earned national attention as one of the fastest-growing cities in the United States, and for good reason. The historic downtown square around the Williamson County Courthouse is genuinely lovely — well-preserved Victorian-era architecture, local restaurants, boutique shops, and community events throughout the year. The city balances its historic character with the explosive growth of developments like Wolf Ranch and the Sun City active adult community.
