💬 About Rock N Grill

Rock N Grill has achieved something remarkable: nearly 9,000 Google reviews at a 4.7-star average, making it one of the highest-reviewed restaurants in all of Central Texas. That level of community trust doesn't happen by accident — it's the result of consistently excellent Indian food, generous portions, attentive service, and a lunch buffet that regulars absolutely swear by. The menu spans the full range of North and South Indian cuisine: creamy butter chicken, aromatic biryanis, perfectly spiced lamb curries, crispy dosas, and an impressive array of vegetarian and vegan options. The lunch buffet is a local institution — reasonably priced, constantly refreshed, and spanning a dozen or more rotating dishes. Dinner service brings an a la carte menu with the same quality in a sit-down dining experience. Located conveniently in Round Rock with easy parking, Rock N Grill serves families, takeout lunch crowds, and date-night diners alike. Whether you're a longtime Indian food lover or trying it for the first time, this is one of the best places to start in Central Texas.
