💬 About Brotherton's Black Iron Barbecue

Brotherton's Black Iron Barbecue has established itself as Pflugerville's definitive barbecue destination, earning a 4.6-star rating from over 1,800 reviews. Pitmaster Jason Brotherton uses post-oak wood and a traditional offset smoker to produce the kind of brisket — dark bark, smoke ring, and melt-in-your-mouth texture — that serious Texas BBQ devotees seek out. The house-made sausage and ribs round out a menu that leans into simplicity and execution. Sides are made from scratch and the beans are the real deal. Like many top Central Texas barbecue joints, Brotherton's operates on limited hours and sells until the meat runs out, so arriving early is strongly recommended. The fact that a neighborhood spot in Pflugerville — not in Austin proper — has built this level of reputation says everything about the quality. It's a must-visit for anyone serious about Texas barbecue in the northern Austin suburbs.
