April 2026 Spotlight: Sandi's Moonwalks

April 1, 2026  ·  Round Rock, TX  ·  Party Rentals
← All Business Spotlights

When it comes to bounce house rentals in Round Rock, one name comes up again and again among parents and event planners across Central Texas: Sandi's Moonwalks. For this month's Business Spotlight, we're putting the well-deserved focus on a company that has quietly built one of the most trusted reputations in the party rental business across the Austin metro.

The Sandi's Standard

What separates Sandi's Moonwalks from the competition in a crowded market? Ask their repeat customers and you'll hear the same things: clean equipment, on-time delivery, and a team that shows up ready to work. In the bounce house business, those three things are everything — and Sandi's gets all three right, consistently.

Every inflatable in the Sandi's fleet is thoroughly cleaned and sanitized between rentals and inspected for safety before every delivery. Commercial-grade blowers keep units properly inflated throughout your event. When the Sandi's truck pulls up to your address, you can expect the team to have the unit up and ready well before your party starts.

Serving All of Central Texas

Based in Round Rock at 2505 Willow Way, Sandi's serves a broad Central Texas footprint — Round Rock, Austin, Pflugerville, Georgetown, Cedar Park, Hutto, Taylor, and beyond. Their central location in the Williamson County area puts them within easy reach of the Austin metro's most active party-planning zip codes.

From intimate backyard birthday parties with a single bounce house to large school carnivals and community events requiring multiple units, Sandi's has the inventory and the experience to deliver at any scale.

Easy Booking

Booking with Sandi's is straightforward — visit sandismoonwalk.com or call (512) 292-2221 to check availability and reserve your date. Weekend slots fill up quickly during peak season — plan ahead for spring and fall events.
