Best Car Washes in Central Texas

Find the best car washes across Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, and all of Central Texas. Express washes, full-service, self-service, and monthly unlimited memberships.

Central Texas is hard on vehicles. Cedar fever coats every car in yellow-green pollen from December through February. Summer dust and limestone construction debris are a constant. A good car wash membership pays for itself. These are the highest-rated options across the region.

Round RockCedar ParkGeorgetownPflugervilleLeanderTaylor
Monthly membership mathAt most express washes, a monthly unlimited membership breaks even after 2–3 washes. During cedar fever season you'll want 6–8.
Free vacuums matterThe best car washes include free, well-maintained vacuum stations. Check this before committing to a membership.
Skip the wax upsellIn-tunnel wax rinses have minimal lasting effect. Save money for a quarterly hand wax if you care about paint protection.
