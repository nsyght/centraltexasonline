November 2025 Spotlight: Waco Bounce House Rentals

November 1, 2025  ·  Eddy, TX  ·  Party Rentals
← All Business Spotlights

Central Texas is a big region, and not every part of it has equal access to quality party rental options. Waco Bounce House Rentals is this month's Business Spotlight — and we're featuring them because they're doing the work of bringing professional inflatable rentals to the Waco metro and McLennan County communities that sit at the northern edge of Central Texas.

Filling the Gap in Waco

Between the Austin metro to the south and the DFW area to the north, Waco has sometimes been underserved by the party rental industry. Waco Bounce House Rentals, based in Eddy, fills that gap directly — serving Waco, Hewitt, Woodway, Bellmead, Hillsboro, McGregor, and McLennan County communities with bounce houses, combo slide units, and obstacle courses.

Who They Serve

Their client mix mirrors the Waco community itself — families hosting birthday parties and residential events, Baylor University-adjacent events and student gatherings, churches running community festivals, schools organizing field days, and businesses planning company picnics. That variety requires a flexible operation and a team that can handle a wide range of event settings. Waco Bounce House Rentals delivers on both counts.

Professional Standards

All inflatables are cleaned, sanitized, and safety-inspected before every rental. Delivery, setup, and pickup are included. Their team is experienced with backyard setups, park pavilions, parking lots, and covered gymnasium installations.

Book Your Waco Event

Call (254) 870-8078 or visit wacobouncehouserentals.com to discuss your event. For weekend bookings, one to two weeks' notice is recommended.
