📅 Central Texas Events

Festivals, farmers markets, outdoor events, and community happenings across Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Killeen, and all of Central Texas.

🌼 Spring & Summer 2026
Annual Festival

Round Rock Express Baseball Season

📅 April – September 2026 📍 Dell Diamond, Round Rock

Triple-A baseball at one of the finest minor league ballparks in the country. The Round Rock Express (Houston Astros affiliate) plays 70+ home games at Dell Diamond. Friday nights feature fireworks after the game. Tickets typically $10–20. Dell Diamond on IH-35 ↗

Nature

Texas Wildflower Season

📅 Late March – April 2026 📍 Burnet County & Hill Country

Central Texas's annual bluebonnet and wildflower bloom — one of the most spectacular natural events in North America. Burnet County holds the Bluebonnet Capital of Texas designation. Best viewing along US-29 west of Georgetown and in the Highland Lakes area. Full wildflower guide ↗

Community

Georgetown Christmas Stroll

📅 November – December 2026 📍 Georgetown Historic Square

Georgetown's historic courthouse square holiday lighting is one of the most spectacular in Central Texas — the city's annual Christmas Stroll draws visitors from across the region. The Victorian architecture and full lighting installation make the square genuinely magical from Thanksgiving through New Year's.

Outdoor

Kalahari Resorts Summer Season

📅 Year-Round (peak June-August) 📍 Round Rock

America's largest indoor waterpark is in Round Rock. Day passes available for non-hotel guests. The indoor waterpark runs year-round — summer adds outdoor pool areas. Birthday packages, group events, and family day trips are popular uses. Book online for best pricing.

Farmers Market

Georgetown Farmers Market

📅 Saturdays, April–November 📍 Downtown Georgetown Square

Georgetown's downtown farmers market on the historic square features local produce, baked goods, artisan crafts, and the community gathering that makes Saturday mornings in Georgetown worth planning around. Pair with a visit to Halcyon Coffee Bar ↗ for the full square experience.

Music & Arts

Waco Cultural Events & Baylor Athletics

📅 Year-Round 📍 Waco / McLane Stadium

Waco's growing cultural calendar includes Baylor football and basketball (McLane Stadium and Foster Pavilion), Magnolia events at the Silos, the Dr Pepper Museum, and a developing arts district. Waco city guide ↗ | Waco visitor's guide ↗

River Recreation

San Marcos River Tubing Season

📅 April – October (peak June-August) 📍 San Marcos River

The spring-fed San Marcos River maintains 72°F year-round — swimmable and tubable in every season. Peak season brings the full social experience. Zentrails Kayak & Tube is the highest-rated outfitter. San Marcos guide ↗

Highland Lakes

Highland Lakes Boating & Recreation Season

📅 April – October 📍 Lake LBJ, Inks Lake & Buchanan

The Highland Lakes chain — Lake LBJ, Inks Lake, Lake Buchanan, Lake Marble Falls — opens the full Central Texas lake recreation season in April. Inks Lake State Park reservations book quickly. Highland Lakes weekend guide ↗

BBQ

Lockhart BBQ — Open Year-Round

📅 Year-Round (lunch hours) 📍 Lockhart, TX (30 mi from Round Rock)

The BBQ Capital of Texas is always open. Kreuz Market, Smitty's Market, Black's Barbecue, and Chisholm Trail BBQ serve the pilgrimage crowd daily. Spring and fall weekends are the best times — arrive at opening. Lockhart BBQ guide ↗
