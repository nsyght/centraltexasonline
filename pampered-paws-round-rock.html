💬 About Pampered Paws Grooming — Round Rock

Pampered Paws Grooming has built Round Rock's top dog grooming reputation, earning 4.9 stars across 1,543 reviews through a combination of genuine skill and the patient, low-stress handling approach that makes a real difference for anxious dogs. The full groom service includes breed-standard or owner-specified haircut, bath with quality shampoo and conditioning treatment, blow-dry, ear cleaning, nail trim, and teeth brushing. The bath and brush service provides a thorough clean and de-shed without the full haircut, suitable for short-coated breeds on a maintenance schedule. Groomers are experienced across the full breed spectrum including the double-coated breeds (Golden Retrievers, Huskies, Aussies) that require specific de-shedding technique and the fine-coated breeds (Bichons, Poodles, Doodles) that require precision cutting. Reviews consistently praise the patience with anxious and senior dogs — a meaningful differentiator when your dog has had bad grooming experiences elsewhere.
