Best Breakfast & Brunch in Central Texas

📅 April 2026  ·  CentralTexas.online Editorial

Central Texas has an excellent breakfast and brunch scene built around both the classic American breakfast institutions and the weekend brunch format that the region's active, food-oriented population has enthusiastically adopted. Here are the best options across the region.

The Best Breakfast and Brunch in Central Texas

🥞 Jack Allen's Kitchen — Round Rock (Best Sunday Brunch)

Jack Allen's Kitchen has built the most anticipated weekly event in Round Rock's dining calendar: Sunday brunch. Bottomless bloody marys, creative brunch dishes rooted in Texas ingredients, and the lively social atmosphere that makes two hours at Jack Allen's feel like a social event rather than just a meal. The Hill Country fried chicken and egg dish is the anchor. The patio is perfect for the Central Texas spring and fall seasons. Waits run 30-45 minutes on peak Sundays — arrive early or embrace the wait as part of the social experience.

🥞 Kerbey Lane Cafe — Round Rock (Best 24-Hour Breakfast)

Kerbey Lane brings the Austin brunch institution's 24-hour breakfast to Round Rock, earning 4.5 stars across 2,343 reviews. The gingerbread pancakes that have been a menu anchor for decades are the signature order. The vegetarian menu is the most comprehensive of any breakfast restaurant in the area. The 24-hour operation covers the early morning need that most Round Rock restaurants ignore. For the 2am meal, the weekday morning before a flight, or the Sunday 7am breakfast before the crowd arrives, Kerbey Lane fills a role no other Round Rock restaurant covers.

Kerbey Lane Cafe — Round Rock4.5 stars, 2,343 reviews. 24-hour breakfast with the gingerbread pancakes. View listing →

🥞 First Watch — Cedar Park (Best Daytime Cafe Breakfast)

First Watch Cedar Park earns 4.5 stars across 2,109 reviews for the daytime-only cafe breakfast that Cedar Park families have made a Saturday morning institution. The fresh-pressed juices are a genuine differentiator. The seasonal menu gives regulars something to explore beyond their usual order. The weekend wait (20-45 minutes on Saturday-Sunday mornings) is real — join the digital waitlist from your car before walking in.

First Watch — Cedar Park4.5 stars, 2,109 reviews. Cedar Park's daytime cafe destination. View listing →

🥞 Original Round Rock Donuts (A Legend)

Not a traditional restaurant, but the Original Round Rock Donuts on Burnet Street deserve their own mention. The giant pink-glazed donuts have been made the same way since 1926 and are one of the genuine food pilgrimages in all of Central Texas. The line on Saturday morning is worth it. Go early — before 9am — for the shortest wait and the freshest donuts.

💡 Breakfast Tips for Central Texas
  • Weekend brunch timing: Central Texas brunch peaks 9:30am-12:30pm on Saturday and Sunday. Arriving before 9am or after 1pm dramatically reduces wait times at popular spots.
  • Digital waitlists: First Watch and many popular brunch spots use Yelp or their own waitlist apps. Join remotely before arriving to reduce in-person waiting time.
  • Original Round Rock Donuts hours: Open from approximately 6am until they sell out, usually by mid-afternoon on weekdays and early afternoon on weekends. The neon pink sign on Burnet Street is the landmark.
