💡 What to Know Before You Book

Book 3–4 weeks outSummer weekends and school event dates fill up fast. The best companies get booked early — especially May through August.
Ask about delivery areaMost Central Texas companies serve a 30–50 mile radius. Always confirm your city is covered before you get attached to a specific unit.
Check insuranceReputable companies carry general liability insurance. Ask for a certificate if your venue requires it — good companies have this ready.
Flat surface mattersInflatables need a flat, clear area free of overhead power lines. Measure your space before booking and share dimensions with the rental company.
Water slides need a hose connectionMake sure you have an outdoor water spigot within reach. Companies typically provide the hose or bring an extension.
Setup is includedEvery company listed here handles delivery and setup. You're not hauling anything. Confirm pickup time so your party schedule works.
