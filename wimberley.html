Wimberley, TX

The Hill Country's most beloved small town — swimming at Blue Hole, cypress-lined Cypress Creek, galleries and antique shops, and the genuine small-town character that people drive from Austin to experience.

🏠 About Wimberley

Wimberley occupies a special place in Central Texas's imagination — the Hill Country small town that actually delivers on the promise. Cypress Creek winds through town, lined with the giant bald cypress trees that give the area its distinctive character. Blue Hole, the beloved spring-fed swimming area, draws swimmers from across the region.

The arts community has been part of Wimberley's identity for decades — galleries, studios, and the Wimberley Market Days (first Saturday of each month) that brings artisans, antique dealers, and food vendors together on the town's main square. The artistic community gives Wimberley a creative energy that most small Texas towns don't have.

Short-term vacation rental properties have made Wimberley an accessible weekend destination for Austin and Central Texas residents. The town manages significant visitor traffic while maintaining the small-town character that makes it worth visiting.
