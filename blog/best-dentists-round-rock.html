What to Look for in a Dentist

  • Google review count, not just rating. A 4.9 from 12 reviews means less than a 4.8 from 2,000 reviews.
  • Insurance acceptance. Call ahead. Most practices accept PPO plans but not all accept HMO or Medicaid.
  • New patient specials. Many Round Rock offices offer free or reduced-cost first exams for new patients.
  • Emergency availability. Ask if they can see you same-day for dental emergencies before you need it.
💡 Local Tip

The best time to schedule a new patient appointment is January through March — most people's insurance deductibles reset and appointments are easier to get.
