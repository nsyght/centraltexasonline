Best Things to Do in Round Rock, TX — 2026 Guide

April 3, 2026  ·  6 min read
← Back to Blog

Round Rock has transformed from a small suburb into one of the most vibrant cities in Central Texas — and it's still growing. Whether you're a new resident or a longtime local looking for something new to discover, here's what's worth your time in Round Rock in 2026.

Catch a Round Rock Express Game

The Round Rock Express plays Triple-A baseball at Dell Diamond, one of the finest minor league ballparks in the country. Great food, low ticket prices, and a beautiful Texas evening under the lights — it's one of Central Texas's best family entertainment values. Check the schedule at milb.com.

Explore Old Settlers Park

Old Settlers Park is Round Rock's crown jewel — 645 acres of trails, sports fields, disc golf, playgrounds, and Henna Lake. The Old Settlers Music Festival every spring is one of Texas's great outdoor traditions. For families planning outdoor events, the park's pavilion rentals paired with equipment from Sandi's Moonwalks or Jump Around Party Rentals make for an unforgettable party.

Shop IKEA Round Rock

IKEA Round Rock is the Central Texas region's furniture and home goods destination. For residents of Austin, Georgetown, Pflugerville, and Cedar Park, it's worth the trip — especially with the famous Swedish meatballs waiting at the end.

See a Live Soccer Match in Austin

Just 30 minutes south, Austin FC at Q2 Stadium brings MLS soccer to the region with an electric atmosphere that Central Texas sports fans have embraced completely. Worth the drive from Round Rock on a match day.
