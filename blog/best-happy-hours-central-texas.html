Best Happy Hours in Central Texas: Round Rock, Cedar Park & Georgetown

📅 April 2026  ·  CentralTexas.online Editorial

Central Texas has a strong happy hour culture, and the best deals in the suburbs are competitive with what Austin offers without the parking situation or the drive. Here are the happy hour programs across the region worth building your weekday evenings around.

Best Happy Hours by City

🍸 Round Rock

Hopdoddy Burger Bar: Weekday happy hour from 4-6pm features $2 off specialty burgers and discounted craft beer. The Tuesday special extends through dinner. For a craft burger and a beer at a genuinely reduced price, Hopdoddy's weekday timing is hard to beat.

Craft Pride: The Texas-only craft beer bar doesn't do formal happy hour pricing — but Wednesday through Thursday evenings have the lowest volume and the most staff time for conversation about the 54 Texas taps. If you want a proper beer education from someone who knows the full board, Wednesday evening is your window.

Jack Allen's Kitchen: Weekday happy hour features $2 off cocktails and beer, half-price select appetizers. The patio at Jack Allen's is excellent for a late-afternoon drink and snack.

Uncorked Wine Bar: Weekday 4-7pm features $2 off wine by the glass and half-price charcuterie plates. The best deal in Round Rock for wine lovers who want a proper evening starter.

🍸 Cedar Park

Blue Sushi Sake Grill: Weekday happy hour 4-6:30pm features significant discounts on specialty rolls and sake. The roll selection at happy hour pricing makes it one of Cedar Park's best food-and-drink value propositions on a weekday evening.

Carrabba's Italian Grill: Tuesday Italiano specials include discounted pasta dishes and a glass of wine. The best weeknight value at a full-service Italian restaurant in the Cedar Park area.

Cedar Park Brewing Company: Wednesday is the local's night with discounted pints. The patio on a Wednesday evening has the neighborhood taproom atmosphere that makes craft brewery happy hours worth adding to the weekly rotation.

🍸 Georgetown

Halcyon Coffee Bar: Transitions to cocktail service at 5pm and runs a cocktail happy hour until 7pm on weekdays. The historic square setting makes Halcyon's happy hour one of the most scenically located in Williamson County.

Szechuan Restaurant: Lunch specials (11am-3pm weekdays) rather than a traditional evening happy hour — but the lunch pricing at Georgetown's best Chinese restaurant is exceptional value and the closest equivalent to happy hour pricing in the area.

💡 Happy Hour Tips for Central Texas
  • Call to confirm: Happy hour programs change seasonally and without public announcement. Call ahead to confirm current times and offers, especially for programs not mentioned on the restaurant's website.
  • Weekday only: Most Central Texas happy hours run Monday-Friday only. Weekend pricing is typically full rate.
  • Patio timing: October-November and March-April are the best months for Central Texas patio happy hours. Summer patio happy hours at 4-5pm are genuinely uncomfortable until the shade comes in.
