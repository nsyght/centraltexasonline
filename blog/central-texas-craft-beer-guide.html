Central Texas Craft Beer Guide: Taprooms, Texas-Only Bars & What to Drink

📅 April 2026  ·  CentralTexas.online Editorial

Central Texas's craft beer scene has matured into something genuinely worth celebrating — locally owned taprooms, a bar that pours exclusively Texas-made beer, and the Austin brewery day-trip circuit that makes the region one of the best craft beer markets in the South. Here's the complete guide.

The Central Texas Taproom Scene

🍻 Oddwood Ales — Round Rock (The Neighborhood Taproom)

Round Rock's highest-rated local brewery at 4.8 stars across 1,234 reviews. Oddwood has built its reputation on small-batch ales of genuine quality, a large dog-friendly outdoor patio, and the neighborhood taproom atmosphere that keeps regulars coming back weekly. The tap list rotates frequently — follow their social channels to see what's pouring before you go. Food trucks rotate through the patio on weekends.

Oddwood Ales — Round Rock4.8 stars, 1,234 reviews. Round Rock's highest-rated local brewery. View listing →

🍺 Craft Pride — Round Rock (The Texas-Only Experience)

54 taps, zero macro beer, every pour a Texas-made craft beer. Craft Pride's rule is absolute and the execution is consistent. The food menu is solid enough to anchor a full evening. If you want to understand the breadth and quality of Texas craft brewing in a single sitting, there is no better venue anywhere in the state.

Craft Pride — Round Rock4.6 stars, 1,876 reviews. 54 Texas-only taps, no exceptions. View listing →

🍻 Cedar Park Brewing Company — Cedar Park (The Family Taproom)

Cedar Park's neighborhood taproom for families and the beer-curious. 4.7 stars, 987 reviews. A wide range of approachable beers alongside more adventurous styles, a family-friendly taproom, and the patio that makes Cedar Park Brewing the default Saturday afternoon gathering place for the 78613 zip code.

Cedar Park Brewing Company — Cedar Park4.7 stars, 987 reviews. Cedar Park's neighborhood taproom. View listing →

🍷 Cedar Ridge Distillery & Winery — Leander (Beyond Beer)

For the spirits enthusiast alongside the beer drinker: Cedar Ridge produces some of Texas's most award-winning bourbon and whiskey, alongside estate wines. 4.7 stars, 1,234 reviews. The beautiful tasting room and outdoor patio make it a destination on its own merits.

Cedar Ridge — Leander4.7 stars, 1,234 reviews. Award-winning Texas whiskey and wine. View listing →

Austin Brewery Day Trips Worth Making

Jester King Brewery (Hill Country outside Austin) — farmhouse ales in a genuinely beautiful setting. Weekend visits require advance planning. The wild fermentation program produces some of the most interesting beers in Texas. Real Ale Brewing in Blanco (90 minutes from Round Rock) is one of Texas's most consistent quality breweries. Celis Brewery in Austin revives the Belgian brewing tradition that made Celis White famous before the original brewery closed.

💡 Central Texas Craft Beer Tips
  • Craft Pride strategy: Ask the bartender for a guided flight organized by style — they'll walk you through the board with genuine knowledge. It's the most educational craft beer experience available in the suburbs.
  • Oddwood food trucks: Check Oddwood's Instagram on Friday afternoons to see which food truck is rotating through the weekend. The truck lineup significantly affects the Saturday experience.
  • Cedar Ridge tasting reservations: Cedar Ridge tasting room can get busy on weekends. Booking a formal tasting in advance (available on their website) guarantees table access and staff attention.
