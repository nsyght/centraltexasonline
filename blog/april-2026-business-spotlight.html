Waco Bounce House Rentals

April 1, 2026  ·  Party & Event Rentals  ·  Eddy, TX
← All Business Spotlights

Our April 2026 Business Spotlight heads north on I-35 to the Waco area — and to Waco Bounce House Rentals, a party rental company serving the greater Central Texas corridor from their base in Eddy, TX. Located between Waco and the broader Austin-Round Rock metro, Waco Bounce House Rentals is uniquely positioned to serve a wide swath of Central Texas communities that don't always have easy access to quality local party rental operators.

Eddy, TX: The Strategic Middle Ground

Eddy sits in Falls County just south of Waco, along the I-35 corridor that connects the Waco area to greater Central Texas. It's an unlikely address for a party rental business — but a strategically smart one. From Eddy, Waco Bounce House Rentals can realistically reach Waco and Hewitt to the north, Temple and Belton to the west, Hillsboro and Corsicana to the east, and even the outer edges of the Austin metro to the south. For communities in that corridor looking for a local party rental option, this is a resource worth knowing about.

The Equipment

Waco Bounce House Rentals maintains a solid inventory of bounce houses in various sizes and themes, combo units with slide and obstacle elements, water slides for warm-weather events, and sports-themed interactive inflatables. Setup and breakdown service is included. For large events requiring multiple units, package pricing is available — call to discuss your specific needs.

Serving the Waco Corridor

For Central Texas communities north of Austin looking for reliable bounce house and party rental service, Waco Bounce House Rentals fills an important gap. Reach them at (254) 870-8078 or visit wacobouncehouserentals.com.
