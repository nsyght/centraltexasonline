Best Steakhouses in Central Texas

📅 April 2026  ·  CentralTexas.online Editorial

A great steakhouse meal is one of Central Texas's quintessential dining experiences — Texas beef country, properly executed, served in an environment that justifies the occasion. Here's where to find the best steaks across the region.

The Best Steakhouses in Central Texas

🥩 Jasper's — Round Rock (Best Fine Dining Steakhouse)

Jasper's brings acclaimed Texas chef Kent Rathbun's Texas Gourmet Backyard cuisine to Round Rock — 4.6 stars, 1,543 reviews, wood-fired steaks and the wine list that makes the price premium worth it. The filet and the prime rib on the bone are the signature items. The patio is exceptional for special occasion dinners that should feel like events. This is the Round Rock steakhouse for anniversaries, promotions, and the meals that mark something significant.

🥩 Lone Star Steaks — Killeen (Best in Bell County)

Lone Star Steaks is Killeen's steakhouse institution, earning 4.5 stars across 1,876 reviews as the celebration dinner destination for Fort Hood retirements, promotions, and homecomings. The steaks are quality-graded, properly aged, and cooked with the consistency that makes a special occasion meal feel genuinely special. The Texas-sized portions and the Lone Star atmosphere match the customer base's expectations perfectly.

🥩 LongHorn Steakhouse — Multiple Locations

LongHorn Steakhouse has earned its reputation in the casual steakhouse segment by delivering consistently cooked steaks at accessible prices, with the fire-grilled flavor that distinguishes them from the boiled and sauced approximations that fill the lower end of the category. The Outlaw Ribeye and the Flo's Filet are the consistent performers. Multiple locations across Central Texas serve the functional steakhouse need without the special occasion pricing.

🥩 Texas Roadhouse — Round Rock & Cedar Park

Texas Roadhouse has built its enormous Central Texas following on the simple proposition: hand-cut steaks, made-from-scratch sides, and the dinner rolls that arrive immediately with cinnamon butter. The wait on Friday evenings is real and worth it. The Ft. Worth Ribeye and the Pull-Apart Rolls deserve their own mention. For families who want a proper steak dinner without the fine dining price, Texas Roadhouse consistently delivers.

💡 Steakhouse Tips
  • Order temperature correctly: Order one temperature level rarer than you think you want. Steakhouses err toward more done — a medium-rare order often arrives medium. Order rare-medium-rare if you prefer medium-rare results.
  • Texas Roadhouse wait management: Call ahead seating is available — call when you're leaving home and your party's name goes on the list. By the time you arrive and check in, the wait is significantly reduced.
  • Jasper's reservation: Jasper's books significantly in advance for Saturday evenings. Call 1-2 weeks ahead for weekend reservations, or take advantage of the typically available Thursday evening tables.
