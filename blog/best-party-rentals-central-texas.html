Booking Tips Specific to Central Texas

  • May through August is peak season. Spring break and end-of-school parties start booking in March. If you have a summer birthday, lock in your rental by April.
  • Texas wind matters. Most companies won't operate inflatables in winds over 20-25 mph. Have a rain/wind policy discussion before you sign. Reputable companies will reschedule, not just keep your deposit.
  • HOA rules vary. Some newer Central Texas neighborhoods have rules about commercial deliveries on weekends. Check with your HOA before booking if you're in a master-planned community.
  • Grass vs. concrete setup. Most inflatables can be staked into grass or weighted down on concrete/asphalt. Tell the company what surface you have — they'll bring the right anchoring equipment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent a bounce house in Central Texas?

Bounce house rentals in Central Texas typically range from $150 to $350 for a standard 4-hour rental, depending on the size of the inflatable and the company. Water slides and obstacle courses run $250 to $500+. Most companies include delivery, setup, and pickup in the price.

How far in advance should I book a bounce house rental?

For summer weekend rentals (May through August), book 3 to 4 weeks in advance. Popular companies like Sandi's Moonwalks and Jump Around Party Rentals fill up quickly for Saturday and Sunday slots during peak season. For weekday or off-season events, 1 to 2 weeks is usually sufficient.

What party rental companies serve Round Rock TX?

Several top-rated companies serve Round Rock: Sandi's Moonwalks, Jump Around Party Rentals, and Hop A Lot Moonwalks are all based in or near Round Rock and serve Williamson County. Austin Water Slide Rentals specializes in water inflatables across the Central Texas area.

Do bounce house rental companies set up and take down?

Yes. Every reputable party rental company in Central Texas handles delivery, setup, and pickup as part of the rental fee. You are not expected to transport or inflate the equipment yourself. Confirm the delivery window and pickup time when you book.
