Hop A Lot Moonwalks

January 1, 2026  ·  Party & Event Rentals  ·  Round Rock, TX
← All Business Spotlights

There's something genuinely satisfying about a family-run business that earns its reputation one event at a time. Hop A Lot Moonwalks, based at 1101 S Industrial Blvd in Round Rock, is exactly that kind of company — owner-operated, community-focused, and built on the simple premise that every event deserves clean, safe, on-time equipment and a crew that treats customers right.

Family Business, Central Texas Values

The family-operated nature of Hop A Lot shapes everything about how they do business. When you call, you talk to the people who own and operate the company — not a call center. When your equipment arrives, it's been inspected by people who care about their reputation, not just their hourly wage. That level of personal accountability is increasingly rare in any service business, and it's one reason Hop A Lot continues to grow its customer base across Central Texas.

What's in the Inventory

Hop A Lot Moonwalks carries bounce houses in multiple themes and sizes, combo inflatables with slides and interactive elements, and obstacle courses that older kids and even adults can't resist. Water slide units are available for warm-weather events — which in Central Texas means most of the year. Setup and teardown are always included in the rental price.

Round Rock's Party Rental Community

Round Rock has an unusually strong concentration of quality party rental companies, and Hop A Lot is a valued part of that community. For customers with large events or specific needs, they'll even refer to other local operators when it's in the customer's best interest. Reach Hop A Lot at (512) 294-2221 or visit hopalotmoonwalks.com.
