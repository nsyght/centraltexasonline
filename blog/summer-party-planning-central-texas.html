Summer Party Planning in Central Texas — How to Beat the Heat

March 15, 2026  ·  6 min read
← Back to Blog

Summer outdoor parties in Central Texas require a different approach than parties in most of the country. When June through September brings consistent 95–105 degree days, you can't just throw up a canopy and call it good. Here's how to plan a summer event that people actually enjoy.

Water Features Are Non-Negotiable

The single best investment for a Central Texas summer party is water. Austin Water Slide Rentals in Pflugerville is the regional specialist — they carry water slides ranging from smaller residential units to massive attractions that work for any age group. Dunk tanks are a bonus for groups where someone's willing to take the plunge.

Time It Right

Schedule outdoor events before 11am or after 5pm. The 11am–4pm window is genuinely brutal and will wear out guests — especially kids — faster than you'd expect. Early morning parties are underrated in Texas.

Bounce Houses Still Work

Even in the heat, bounce houses are crowd-pleasers — just set them up in shade if possible and make sure there's water access nearby. Sandi's Moonwalks, Jump Around Party Rentals, and Hop A Lot Moonwalks all serve the Austin metro with clean, safe inflatable rentals.

For Williamson County and Rural Events

If you're hosting on acreage or a rural property, We Bring The Party, LLC in Florence is the team to call — they specialize in deliveries that other companies won't attempt.
