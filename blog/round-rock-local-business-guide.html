Round Rock, TX Local Business Guide 2026

📅 2026  ·  CentralTexas.online
← Back to Blog

Round Rock has gone from a small city north of Austin to one of the fastest-growing communities in the United States — and with that growth has come a thriving local business scene. Here's the guide to what's worth knowing in Round Rock.

The Food Icons

Round Rock Donuts is not optional. This Texas institution has been making Texas-sized glazed donuts since 1926 and has earned its reputation as the best donut in the state. Arrive before 9am on weekends or you'll be choosing from what's left. The Salt Lick BBQ at Dell Diamond brings Central Texas's most famous BBQ brand to a location that's easy to reach from anywhere in the metro.

Shopping

Round Rock Premium Outlets is the region's destination for outlet shopping — 125+ stores with real savings right off I-35. It draws shoppers from throughout Central Texas and is particularly popular during tax-free weekends.

Services

Round Rock Family Dentistry is accepting new patients and covers the full range of family dental care. Central Texas Lawn & Landscaping handles Round Rock's Bermuda and St. Augustine grass with the expertise that Central Texas soil demands.

Party Rentals

Round Rock is the party rental capital of Central Texas — three established operators call it home: Sandi's Moonwalks, Jump Around Party Rentals, and Hop A Lot Moonwalks.
