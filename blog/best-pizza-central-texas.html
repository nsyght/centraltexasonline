Best Pizza in Central Texas: Beyond the Chains

📅 April 2026  ·  CentralTexas.online Editorial

Central Texas's pizza scene has evolved well beyond the delivery chains, though the chains remain important for the specific role they fill. Here's the guide to the best pizza across the region — from Neapolitan wood-fired pies to New York slices to the unexpected regional styles that have found homes in Williamson County.

The Best Pizza Restaurants in Central Texas

🍕 Maccheroni Republic — Round Rock (Best Overall)

Maccheroni Republic is simultaneously Round Rock's best pasta restaurant and its most underrated pizza destination. The wood-fired pizzas are executed with the Neapolitan-influenced technique that produces a properly charred, slightly chewy crust with quality toppings. The BYOB policy makes a pizza dinner at Maccheroni genuinely exceptional value — bring your own wine and get restaurant-quality Neapolitan pizza without the wine markup. 4.7 stars, 1,654 reviews.

🍕 The Heights Pizzeria — Harker Heights (Drive Worth Making)

Technically in Harker Heights (Killeen area), The Heights Pizzeria has earned national pizza recognition with a 4.9-star rating across 5,759 reviews — one of the highest-rated restaurants of any category in all of Central Texas. The New York-style pies have earned devoted fans who drive significant distances for a regular order. If you're passing through Bell County, this is the stop that justifies a detour.

🍕 Conan's Pizza — Round Rock & Taylor

Conan's Pizza is the Austin-born pizza institution that Central Texas grew up with — a chain in the best sense, meaning consistent quality delivered with the care of an institution that has been making pizza since 1976. The thick, chewy crust and generous toppings have made Conan's the pizza that Round Rock and Taylor residents reach for when they want something more substantial than a delivery chain pie.

🍕 Domino's & Pizza Hut: The Delivery Anchors

For late-night delivery and the reliable familiar experience, Domino's Pflugerville (4.1 stars, 1,234 reviews) and Pizza Hut Killeen (4.0 stars, 1,543 reviews) serve the functional delivery need that no artisan pizza restaurant replaces. The technology innovations — Domino's tracker, online ordering — make these the most frictionless pizza delivery experiences available.

💡 Pizza Tips for Central Texas
  • Maccheroni BYOB: Bring a bottle of Italian red — Chianti, Barbera, or a Montepulciano pairs perfectly with wood-fired pizza. The BYOB policy at Maccheroni transforms a nice pizza dinner into a genuinely special meal at no additional cost.
  • New York vs Neapolitan: New York style has a thin, pliable crust that folds. Neapolitan has a thicker, charred, chewy rim with a thinner center. The Heights does New York-style. Maccheroni does Neapolitan-influenced. Both are excellent; they're different experiences.
  • Pizza night planning: Maccheroni Republic books up on Friday-Saturday evenings. Call ahead or use their reservation system if you have a specific night in mind.
