About Pflugerville

Pflugerville sits in a sweet spot northeast of Austin — close enough for easy commuting, far enough for affordable housing and a distinct community identity. The city is served by Pflugerville ISD, known for strong academics and athletics. Lake Pflugerville is the crown jewel of the parks system. The business corridor along FM 685 and Pecan Street continues to grow with both national retailers and standout local businesses.
