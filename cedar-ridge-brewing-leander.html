💬 About Cedar Ridge Brewing Company — Leander

Cedar Ridge Brewing & Distilling has built one of the most celebrated craft spirits and wine operations in Central Texas, earning 4.7 stars across 1,234 reviews by producing genuinely award-winning Texas whiskey and estate wines that have elevated the state's craft beverage reputation nationally. The whiskey program is the headline: the Straight Bourbon Whiskey has won multiple national awards and regularly appears on 'best Texas whiskey' lists by spirits writers who cover the category seriously. The single malts and experimental cask-aged expressions give the serious whiskey enthusiast something to explore beyond the core lineup. The estate wine program produces Texas-grown wines including Tempranillo, Cabernet Sauvignon, and white varietals that reflect the Hill Country terroir. The tasting room experience is well-designed: flights that introduce both the spirits and wine programs, knowledgeable staff, and the outdoor patio that makes a late afternoon visit genuinely beautiful. For Leander and 183A corridor residents who want a craft beverage destination without driving to the Hill Country wine country, Cedar Ridge provides that experience at home.
