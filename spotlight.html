Get Your Business Spotlighted

Every month, CentralTexas.online features one local Central Texas business in an in-depth spotlight article — celebrating the entrepreneurs and teams that make this region great. Spotlights are free, permanent, and include a dedicated article, featured homepage placement, and a lasting presence in our directory.

How the Spotlight Works
1

Nomination

Any business in Central Texas can be nominated — self-nominations are welcome. Tell us what makes your business worth featuring.

2

Selection

We review nominations and select one business per month based on community connection, story, and local impact.

3

Feature

Your business gets a dedicated spotlight article, featured placement on our homepage, and a permanent listing on this page.

4

Forever

Spotlight articles never expire. Your feature lives permanently on CentralTexas.online and ranks in search results.
