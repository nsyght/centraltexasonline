Best Family Activities in Central Texas: Year-Round Guide

📅 April 2026  ·  CentralTexas.online Editorial

Central Texas is one of the best places in the country to raise a family — excellent schools, outdoor access, a growing entertainment infrastructure, and the Texas character that makes community life genuinely feel like community. Here are the best family activities across the region.

Year-Round Family Favorites

⚾ Round Rock Express Baseball (April–September)

Dell Diamond in Round Rock hosts the Express, the Houston Astros' Triple-A affiliate, in one of the finest minor league ballparks in the United States. Tickets run $10–20, parking is easy, the food selection is good, and Friday night fireworks make the experience a full event. Bring a blanket and arrive early for batting practice — players are often close to fans during warm-ups. This is one of the best family entertainment values in all of Central Texas.

🌊 Kalahari Resorts — Round Rock

The largest indoor waterpark in America is in Round Rock and operates year-round. Day passes for the waterpark allow families to visit without the hotel stay. The indoor slides, lazy river, wave pool, and the outdoor areas (open spring through fall) make Kalahari the most comprehensive single-site family activity in the region. Birthday parties and group events are a significant part of the business.

🎮 EVO Entertainment — Hutto

Bowling, movies, laser tag, arcade games, and billiards all under one roof at EVO Entertainment in Hutto, earning 4.5 stars across 1,234 reviews. The combination of activities under one roof makes it the most practical venue for birthday parties and family group events. Full restaurant and bar service means no one has to leave for dinner.

⛳ Big Shot Indoor Golf — Cedar Park

Indoor golf simulators that the whole family can enjoy regardless of handicap — Big Shot earns 4.7 stars in Cedar Park for the year-round golf simulator experience. Kids can compete with parents, non-golfers can participate meaningfully, and the food and beverage service makes it a full evening. Perfect for rainy days or summer heat when outdoor golf isn't comfortable.

Outdoor Family Activities

🌳 Brushy Creek Regional Trail — Round Rock & Cedar Park

Fifteen miles of paved trail through the Brushy Creek watershed, connecting parks across the corridor. The trail is excellent for family bike rides, runs, and dog walks. Multiple entry points with parking. The creek crossings and wooded sections create an outdoor experience that feels natural rather than suburban despite its location.

🧫 Longhorn Cavern State Park — Burnet

Guided cave tours through one of Texas's most spectacular limestone cave systems earn 4.6 stars across 3,876 reviews. The cave maintains a constant 68°F year-round — perfect when Central Texas heat makes outdoor activities uncomfortable. Kids are consistently described as genuinely captivated. The drive (70 miles from Round Rock) is a reasonable day trip that extends into a full Highland Lakes exploration.

🛶 San Marcos River Tubing (April–October)

The spring-fed San Marcos River at constant 72°F is one of Central Texas's most beloved family experiences. Zentrails Kayak & Tube provides tube and kayak rentals with shuttle service. The gentle current and shallow sections make it appropriate for children who can swim. The extraordinary water clarity makes it unlike any other Central Texas water experience.

💡 Family Activity Tips for Central Texas
  • Indoor backup plan: Central Texas summer heat (100°F+ days, June-August) requires an indoor backup for outdoor plans. EVO Entertainment and Kalahari are the primary options when it's too hot for outdoor activities.
  • Hat Creek Burger Company: The Cedar Park location has the best restaurant playground in the area — parents get to actually eat while kids play. The combination of good burgers and kid-friendly space makes it the default family dinner answer.
  • Dell Diamond timing: Friday night games sell out faster than weeknight games due to the post-game fireworks. For guaranteed seating at a fireworks game, buy tickets online in advance.
