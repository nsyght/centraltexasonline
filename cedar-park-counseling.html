💬 About Cedar Park Counseling Center

Cedar Park Counseling Center has built the 183A corridor's top mental health practice rating, earning 4.8 stars across 432 reviews by delivering quality therapy that addresses the specific stressors of Cedar Park and Leander's high-achieving, dual-income family population. The practice offers individual therapy, couples counseling, family therapy, and child and adolescent therapy — a comprehensive range that allows whole families to receive care at a single practice. Child therapy is a particular strength: therapists trained in play therapy and child-specific techniques who can work with children as young as four years old. The common presenting concerns reflect the Cedar Park demographic: parenting stress, school anxiety and performance pressure, marital stress related to dual-career demands, and the general anxiety that the high-pace suburban tech corridor produces. Most major insurance plans are accepted including Aetna, BCBS, Cigna, and United. Telehealth is available for all service types.
