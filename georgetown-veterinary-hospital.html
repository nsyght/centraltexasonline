💬 About Georgetown Veterinary Hospital

Georgetown Veterinary Hospital has been a cornerstone of pet care for the Williamson County community, earning a 4.8-star Google rating from over 1,530 pet owners. The practice provides comprehensive veterinary medicine — from routine wellness exams and vaccinations to diagnostics, dental care, surgery, and end-of-life services — all under one roof. Pet owners consistently note that veterinarians here take their time explaining diagnoses and treatment options rather than rushing through appointments, and that the staff treats animals with genuine care and compassion. The hospital maintains a strong relationship with the Georgetown community and has cared for generations of pets for many long-term clients. Morning hours starting at 7:30am and Saturday clinic hours make it accessible for working pet owners. For Georgetown families looking for a vet they can trust through every chapter of their pet's life, Georgetown Veterinary Hospital has earned that trust from more than 1,500 families who've walked through its doors. Welcoming new patients — call to schedule a new patient exam.
