💬 About Waterloo Ice House — Round Rock

Waterloo Ice House brings the classic Austin ice house tradition to Round Rock's historic downtown area, earning 4.3 stars across 1,543 reviews as the neighborhood bar and grill that captures the easy-going Texas outdoor drinking and eating culture. The beer selection emphasizes Texas craft brews on draft alongside the familiar Texas standards. The burger program is solid — made-to-order with quality ingredients in the format that pairs naturally with a cold beer on a warm Texas evening. The outdoor patio is the heart of the experience: string lights, communal tables, and the relaxed atmosphere that makes a Tuesday after work feel like a social event. Bar food covers the full range of patio-eating staples — chicken tenders, queso, tacos, wings. The Old Town Round Rock location puts it in the most walkable and charming part of the city. For Round Rock residents who want the ice house experience without driving to Austin, Waterloo delivers the authentic version.
