Best Urgent Care Clinics in Central Texas

Find the top-rated walk-in urgent care clinics in Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, and Killeen. No appointment needed, X-ray on-site, and most insurance accepted.

Urgent care fills the gap between your primary care doctor and the ER for conditions that need attention today but aren't life-threatening emergencies. Using urgent care appropriately can save you hours and hundreds of dollars compared to an ER visit for the same condition.

Round RockCedar ParkGeorgetownKilleenPflugerville
Use online check-inAll major urgent care chains have online check-in. Use it. Checking in from home and arriving when your time approaches cuts your wait significantly.
Urgent care vs ERUse urgent care for: flu, strep, ear infections, sprains, minor cuts, UTIs, COVID testing. Use ER for: chest pain, stroke symptoms, severe allergic reactions, major injuries.
TRICARE acceptedMost Central Texas urgent care clinics accept TRICARE. Confirm when you check in - active duty military may have different requirements.
