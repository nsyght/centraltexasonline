💬 About La Margarita Mexican Restaurant — Round Rock

La Margarita has carved out a loyal following in Round Rock's competitive Tex-Mex landscape, earning 4.4 stars across 1,876 reviews by delivering reliably good Tex-Mex food in a comfortable, family-oriented setting. The fajita program is the anchor: chicken, beef, and combination platters that sizzle properly when they arrive, with the full accompaniment of warm tortillas, guacamole, sour cream, pico de gallo, and cheese. The margarita menu is extensive and the quality is consistent — frozen or on the rocks, house or premium tequila, the right level of sour-to-sweet balance. Combination plates cover the full Tex-Mex catalogue: enchiladas, tacos, chalupas, chiles rellenos. Portions are generous. The east Round Rock location on US-79 gives it convenient access for residents in Hutto and eastern Williamson County who want Tex-Mex close to home.
