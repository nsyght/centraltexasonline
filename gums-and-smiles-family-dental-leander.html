💬 About Gums and Smiles Family Dental

Gums and Smiles Family Dental has become one of Leander's most trusted healthcare providers, building a 4.9-star Google rating from over 1,000 patient reviews. As Leander has transformed into one of the fastest-growing cities in Texas, the demand for reliable, high-quality local dental care has surged — and Gums and Smiles has met it with comprehensive family dentistry that covers every stage of life. The practice offers general preventive care, restorative services, cosmetic dentistry, and orthodontic consultations, making it a genuine one-stop dental home for families. Saturday hours set the practice apart from many competitors, making it significantly easier for working parents to bring children in without taking weekdays off. Patient reviews consistently highlight the friendliness of the staff, the thoroughness of cleanings, and the transparency in treatment planning and billing. For families who have recently moved to Leander and the Crystal Falls, Travisso, or Devine Lake communities, establishing care here comes with over 1,000 five-star endorsements from your neighbors.
