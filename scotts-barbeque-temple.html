💬 About Scott's Bar-B-Que — Temple

Scott's Bar-B-Que has built Temple's most devoted BBQ following, earning 4.6 stars across 1,432 reviews through decades of consistent Central Texas-style pit barbecue that serves the Bell County community with the reliability that a genuinely great neighborhood BBQ institution provides. The brisket is the standard-bearer — smoked properly with the bark development and smoke ring that distinguishes real pit BBQ from the approximation. Pork ribs and house sausage round out the core menu. Sides are made in-house: pinto beans cooked low and slow, coleslaw with the right balance of creamy and tangy, and the potato salad that regulars have been ordering alongside their brisket for years. The atmosphere is the authentic Central Texas BBQ joint experience — no frills, no performance, just good food served by people who take it seriously. For Bell County and Temple residents who want outstanding BBQ without making the Lockhart drive, Scott's provides the answer that earns genuine community pride.
