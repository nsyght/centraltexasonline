Moving to Central Texas? Here's What You Need to Know (2026)

📅 2026  ·  CentralTexas.online

Welcome to One of America's Fastest-Growing Regions

Central Texas has been one of the top relocation destinations in the United States for most of the past decade. The combination of no state income tax, relatively affordable housing (compared to coastal metros), a booming job market, warm weather, and genuine quality of life has made the Austin metro and surrounding communities a magnet for families, remote workers, and businesses alike.

If you're moving here — or thinking about it — this guide covers what you actually need to know. The practical stuff. Where to live, what to expect, and how to find the services that matter.

Choosing Where to Live

The Austin metro is sprawling, and each suburb has a distinct character. Here's the honest breakdown:

⭕ Round Rock

The largest Williamson County city and home to Dell Technologies. Round Rock has the best combination of infrastructure, schools (Round Rock ISD), amenities, and proximity to Austin. Downtown is reviving. Dell Diamond baseball adds genuine community character. Housing is more expensive than outer suburbs but more accessible than Austin proper. Explore Round Rock businesses.

🌱 Georgetown

One of the most beautiful downtowns in Texas. Williamson County seat. Georgetown is experiencing enormous growth but has preserved its historic character well. Sun City makes it a top retirement destination. Strong schools (Georgetown ISD and some Leander ISD). Explore Georgetown businesses.

🌲 Cedar Park

Consistently ranked as one of the best places to live in Texas. Leander ISD (one of the state's top districts). Beautiful parks system including Brushy Creek. Slightly more expensive than Pflugerville or Hutto but the infrastructure and schools justify it for many families. Explore Cedar Park businesses.

🌊 Pflugerville

The most affordable of the major Williamson/Travis County suburbs. Excellent value. Growing business corridor. Pflugerville ISD. Lake Pflugerville is a genuine gem. Good for families who want Austin proximity without the Austin price tag. Explore Pflugerville businesses.

⚡ Taylor

One to watch. Samsung's $17 billion semiconductor plant is transforming Taylor's economy. Historic downtown is charming. Housing is still relatively affordable. If you work in tech or manufacturing and want space, Taylor is worth serious consideration.

The Services You'll Need First

Grocery

H-E-B is the dominant and genuinely beloved Texas grocery institution. There's one in almost every Central Texas city. Find your nearest H-E-B first — it will become a weekly ritual.

🛒

H-E-B Round Rock

Central Texas's most beloved grocery store. Meal Simple prepared meals, excellent produce, curbside pickup, and a level of Texas pride you have to experience.
