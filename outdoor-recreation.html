Best Outdoor Recreation in Central Texas

Find the best outdoor activities, parks, rivers, and nature destinations across Central Texas — from the San Marcos River to Inks Lake State Park to the Highland Lakes.

Central Texas has extraordinary natural assets that residents often underutilize — spring-fed rivers, state parks, Highland Lakes, Hill Country terrain, and an outdoor recreation season that runs most of the year. Here are the top outdoor destinations across the region.

Marble FallsSan MarcosGeorgetownRound RockBurnet
San Marcos River temperatureThe San Marcos River maintains 72°F year-round due to the springs that feed it. Perfect for summer tubing — genuinely refreshing even in August heat.
Inks Lake State Park reservationsBook at texasstateparks.reserveamerica.com at least 3-4 weeks ahead for spring/fall weekends. Same-day availability is rare.
Hill Country winery weekendFall Creek and Stone House Vineyard in the Marble Falls area are both worth visiting in the same day — they're within 15 minutes of each other.
