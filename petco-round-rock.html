💬 About Petco — Round Rock

Petco Round Rock serves Williamson County's large pet-owning community with the full Petco experience, earning 4.3 stars across 1,234 reviews. The product selection covers dogs, cats, fish, birds, reptiles, and small animals with food, supplies, toys, and specialty items for each. The in-store grooming salon handles everything from basic bath and brush services to full grooming for all breeds. The Vetco vaccination clinics operate regularly within the store for routine vaccinations at accessible pricing — a practical option for owners whose pets are healthy and just need annual shots. Training classes for dogs operate through the store's training program. The Gattis School Road location is convenient to Round Rock's residential neighborhoods and is one of the highest-traffic pet supply destinations in Williamson County. For Round Rock pet owners who need pet food, supplies, grooming, or basic veterinary services, Petco handles the full range.
