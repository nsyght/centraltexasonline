💬 About Waco Bounce House Rentals

Waco Bounce House Rentals serves the Waco metro area and McLennan County with a comprehensive selection of inflatable bounce houses, combo slide units, obstacle courses, and party rental equipment. Based in Eddy, Texas, they're positioned to cover Waco, Hewitt, Woodway, Bellmead, Hillsboro, McGregor, and the broader McLennan County region.

Waco sits at the northern edge of the Central Texas region, and Waco Bounce House Rentals fills a real need for the communities between the Austin metro and the DFW area. Their clients include families hosting birthday parties and backyard events, churches running community festivals, schools organizing field days and carnivals, and businesses planning company picnics.

All inflatables are thoroughly cleaned, sanitized, and safety-inspected before every rental. Delivery, setup, and pickup are included in all rental packages. Their team is experienced with a variety of event settings — residential backyards, park pavilions, parking lots, and indoor gymnasium setups when appropriate.

Call (254) 870-8078 to check availability and pricing for your Waco area event. For weekend events, booking one to two weeks in advance is recommended. They serve a broad area across McLennan County and are willing to discuss delivery for events in the greater Central Texas corridor.
