The Wedding Capital of Texas — Hill Country gateway with award-winning distilleries, stunning venues, and the natural beauty that draws visitors from across Central Texas.

🏠 About Dripping Springs

Dripping Springs has earned its reputation as the Wedding Capital of Texas through a combination of stunning Hill Country setting, an exceptional concentration of wedding venues, and the full ecosystem of wedding vendors that serves the thousands of ceremonies held here annually. The limestone terrain, live oak groves, and panoramic Hill Country views create natural backdrops that draw couples from across Texas and beyond.

Beyond weddings, Dripping Springs has become the Hill Country gateway city for the western Austin corridor — the starting point for Hill Country exploration, a growing food and beverage scene anchored by award-winning distilleries, and a community that has maintained its character while experiencing significant growth from Austin spillover.

Dripping Springs ISD is a small, well-regarded district. The community's strong agricultural and outdoor recreation heritage remains present alongside the new development.
