🤠 About Central Texas

Central Texas stretches from Waco in the north to San Marcos in the south, with Austin as the hub. It encompasses the Austin metro, Williamson County (Round Rock, Georgetown, Cedar Park, Leander, Hutto, Taylor, Florence), Bell County (Killeen, Temple, Belton), and McLennan County (Waco). No state income tax, warm weather year-round, and a strong job market make it one of the most popular relocation destinations in the U.S.
