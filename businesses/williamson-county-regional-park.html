💬 About Williamson County Regional Park

Williamson County Regional Park in Cedar Park is one of the most significant recreation and sports facilities in Central Texas. The 137-acre park campus includes expansive athletic fields for soccer, softball, football, and other sports; an outdoor amphitheater hosting concerts and community events; playground areas; walking and jogging trails; and the H-E-B Center at Cedar Park — a 7,000-seat arena that hosts the AHL's Texas Stars, the NWSL's Austin FC development events, concerts, family shows, and major sporting events. The park is a focal point for youth and adult sports leagues throughout Williamson County and attracts visitors from across the Austin metro for major events at the arena.
