💬 About Round Rock Express

The Round Rock Express has been the heart of Central Texas baseball since 2000, playing at the beautiful Dell Diamond in Round Rock. As the Triple-A affiliate of a major league franchise, the Express showcases tomorrow's MLB stars today at prices that keep it accessible for the whole family. The ballpark experience features outstanding concessions, a playground, a giant home run porch, and one of the best fireworks shows in the region. Checking the schedule and picking up tickets online is the best way to catch a game.
