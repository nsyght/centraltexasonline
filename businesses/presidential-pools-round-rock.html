💬 About Presidential Pools & Spas — Round Rock

Presidential Pools & Spas has built Round Rock's strongest pool company reputation, earning 4.7 stars across 1,654 reviews by delivering excellence across the full pool ownership lifecycle: construction, maintenance, and renovation. Custom pool construction is the anchor — the design team works with homeowners to create pool and spa combinations that fit the lot, the budget, and the lifestyle. The maintenance division handles ongoing weekly chemical service, keeping pool water balanced through the dramatic temperature swings that Central Texas summers produce. Equipment repair and replacement covers pumps, filters, heaters, and automation systems. Pool renovation addresses aging surfaces, outdated equipment, and the aesthetic updates that give an older pool a new life. Central Texas's extended warm season — genuinely swim-able weather from April through October — makes pool ownership one of the highest-use amenities in the region's housing market.
