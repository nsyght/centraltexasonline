Best Mexican Restaurants in Central Texas

Find the best Mexican restaurants and taquerias across Round Rock, Georgetown, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Leander, Hutto, and Kyle TX. Tex-Mex, breakfast tacos, tamales, and margaritas.

Central Texas has a thriving Mexican food scene that ranges from old-school Tex-Mex institutions serving margaritas to generations of families, to authentic taquerias open from 7am for breakfast tacos. These are the highest-rated, most-reviewed Mexican restaurants across the region.

Order the breakfast tacosTaquerias like La Hacienda in Georgetown are open early. Breakfast taco runs are a Central Texas morning ritual.
Ask about daily specialsAuthentic taquerias rotate specials based on what's fresh. The best items often aren't on the printed menu.
Margarita happy hoursMost Tex-Mex spots offer weekday happy hour deals. Usually 3–6pm with $1–2 off margaritas.
