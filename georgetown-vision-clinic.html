💬 About Georgetown Vision Clinic

Georgetown Vision Clinic serves one of the most demographically diverse communities in Central Texas - a city that includes both rapidly growing family neighborhoods and Sun City, one of the largest active adult communities in Texas. The 4.8-star average across 876 reviews reflects a practice built competency across that spectrum. Macular degeneration screening, glaucoma management, diabetic eye care, and cataract evaluation referrals are offered alongside standard refractions. The contact lens program handles both standard soft lenses and specialty fits including multifocal contacts and specialty lenses for astigmatism. Georgetown Vision Clinic accepts most major vision insurance plans.
